Point of view

If you wait until it's ready, you're already too late.

In long, capital-intensive development projects, every cycle and conversation counts. The market story has to grow alongside your core tech - not just get bolted on at the very end.

Finding partners

A partner narrative opens early doors that cold outreach never will.

Attracting talent

Mission and story are part of recruiting and compensation strategy.

Extending the runway

A clear, distinct investor narrative helps you raise faster, on better terms.

Understanding the market

Customer and competitor research shapes what you build, not just how to sell.

Crafting a narrative

Adjacent industries, regulators, acquirers - each needs a version of the story.

Building early social proof

Case studies can take years to build. Start as early as possible.

Marketing can't fix underlying technology, your org design, or any legal / IP problems. But a GTM voice belongs in the room early - especially as your product is still being built.
